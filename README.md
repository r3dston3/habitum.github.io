This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.



1\. Information Collection and Use



Habitum does not collect, store, use, or share any personal data from users.



The App:



* does not require user registration



* does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account information



* does not access location data



* does not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or advertising IDs



2\. Third-Party Services



The App does not use any third-party services or SDKs that collect data from users.



3\. Internet Connection



Habitum works fully offline and does not require an internet connection.



4\. Data Security



Since no personal or device data is collected or processed, there is no risk of such data being stored, shared, or exposed.



5\. Children’s Privacy



The App does not knowingly collect any information from children or adults. As no data collection occurs, the App complies with applicable child privacy regulations.
